  • Plate 2:

1.3 Observations

  • Control rxns with FLY primers show expected results
  • In control rxn with 100 ng of DNA extracted from known positive flies, she still can barely see the band when using the TRYP primers
  • It is possible that some false negatives exist in these data
  • Further supports abandoning the pooled PCR rxns for individual rxns
